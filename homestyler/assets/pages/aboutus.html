      ABOUT US

      ADDING THE EXTRAORDINARY TO EVERYDAY LIFE

      At Home Styler we bring together the finest materials, traditional artisanal craftsmanship, and an approach that’s wholly bespoke to your ideas to create the best in British luxury interiors.

      OUR BELIEFS

      We believe in helping you define your dreams, before realising that inspiration with care and imagination. It’s about fusing your desires, tastes and needs with modern design innovation and traditional craftsman’s passion for perfection. By collaborating creatively, we will make the setting for your everyday truly extraordinary.

      OUR CLIENTS

      Our clients are as diverse as the dreams they share with us. For us, it’s about saying yes to possibility and thinking far beyond the confines of convention. We embrace individuality, eclecticism and whimsy, adding creativity and the very best of British craftsmanship to create truly special places to live.

      OUR COLLECTIONS

      From the contemporary to the traditional, the unparalleled design of our collections will bring opulence and creativity to your home. Whether it’s a kitchen, bedroom, dressing room or an office, we’ll help you to create the ultimate space to entertain, relax and elevate the everyday.

      CRAFTMANSHIP

      Our workshop in the north of England is a UK Centre of Excellence. There, generations of artisan craftsmen and women combine precision technology with 300 year-old cabinetry and finishing techniques. It’s home to some of the finest skilled artisans currently working today. This is where we continue to create exquisite furniture that will bring warmth and life to your living space for generations to come.

      We take great pride in choosing - and using - only the finest materials. Our team are tasked with continually discovering and responsibly sourcing ever-more unique materials from around the world, ready to perfect every creation. Our veneers are equally at home in a Bentley or Rolls Royce, as they are gracing our cabinetry. Furthermore, where possible, we support small UK manufacturers who - like us - have a ‘no compromise’ policy when it comes to quality.
