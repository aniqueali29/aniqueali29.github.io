TECHNICAL SERVICES

LASER THERAPY

Laser therapy uses focused light to treat various medical and cosmetic conditions by targeting specific tissues or cells.

MASSAGE THERAPY

Massage therapy involves the manipulation of muscles and tissues to relieve pain, reduce stress, and improve overall well-being.

OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY

Occupational therapy helps individuals develop, recover, or maintain the skills needed for daily living and work activities.

ELECTROTHERAPY

Electrotherapy uses electrical currents to alleviate pain, enhance tissue healing, and stimulate muscle activity for various therapeutic benefits.

NUTRITIONAL THERAPY

Nutritional therapy involves using dietary changes and supplements to improve health and manage various conditions.

CHIROPRACTIC CARE

Chiropractic care focuses on diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal disorders, primarily through spinal adjustments to improve alignment and relieve pain.

JOINT MOBILIZATION

Joint mobilization is a manual therapy technique that involves gently moving a joint to improve its range of motion and function.

ARTICULATION THERAPY

Articulation therapy helps individuals improve their speech clarity by addressing issues with the articulation of sounds.

BIOFEEDBACK THERAPY

Biofeedback therapy uses devices to monitor and help control physiological functions, like stress and muscle tension.
