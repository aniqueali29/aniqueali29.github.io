Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Zara Therapy Clinic offer?

Zara Therapy Clinic offers a range of therapy services including physical therapy, mental health counseling, and holistic wellness programs.

How can I book an appointment?

Appointments can be booked online through our website or by calling our clinic directly during business hours.

What are your operating hours?

Our clinic operates Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact us to confirm if your insurance is accepted.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance information, and any relevant medical records or referral notes.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

How can I contact the clinic for more information?

You can reach us via email at contact@zaratherapy.com or call us at (123) 456-7890.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we offer online consultations via video conferencing for those unable to visit the clinic in person.
