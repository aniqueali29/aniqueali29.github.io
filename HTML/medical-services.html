MEDICAL SERVICES

PHYSIOTHERAPY

Physiotherapy, or physical therapy, is a healthcare profession focused on diagnosing, managing, and treating musculoskeletal and movement disorders.

PSYCHOTHERAPY

Psychotherapy is a treatment for mental health issues involving talking with a therapist to understand and improve thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.

ROBOTIC REHABILITATION

Robotic rehabilitation uses robotic devices to assist and enhance the recovery of physical abilities in individuals with impairments or disabilities.

SHOCKWAVE THERAPY

Shockwave therapy uses high-energy acoustic waves to stimulate healing and reduce pain in soft tissues and joints.

ULTRASOUND THERAPY

Ultrasound therapy uses sound waves to create deep heat in tissues, improving blood flow and accelerating healing for injuries and muscle pain.

VIRTUAL REALITY THERAPY

Virtual reality therapy uses immersive VR to create simulated environments for treating conditions like anxiety, PTSD, and chronic pain.

ACUPUNCTURE

Acupuncture involves inserting thin, sterile needles into specific points on the body to relieve pain, balance energy flow, and improve overall health.

CARDIAC REHABILITATION

Cardiac rehabilitation is a supervised program that helps individuals recover from heart-related conditions through exercise, education, and lifestyle changes.

SWALLOWING THERAPY

Swallowing therapy helps improve safe and effective swallowing through exercises and techniques that strengthen and coordinate the muscles involved.
